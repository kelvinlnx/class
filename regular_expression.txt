Regular Expressions
===================

1. Wildcards
      . = any single character
      [abc] = a or b or c (case sensitive)
      [abcABC] = a or b or c or A or B or C
      [^abc] = NOT a or b or c
      [a-z]  = a or b or c.... or z
2. Modifiers
      * - zero or more of previous atom(character)
            Eg.  a*  - represents NOTHING or a or aa or aaa or aaaa ....
                 [abc]* - represents NOTHING or a string of characters made out or a OR/AND b OR/AND c
      + - 1 or more of previous atom
      ? - 0 or 1 of previous atom
            Eg. A1? - A or A1
      [x[,y]] - represents as below
        [x] - exactly X of previous atom
        [x,] - X or More of previous atom
        [x,y] - X to Y or previous atom
            Eg.  w[3]  - www
                [0-9][2,5] - 00 ... 99999
3. Anchor
      Symbol appears before atom
      --------------------------
      ^ - Line starts with atom
      \< - Word starts with atom
      
      Symbol appears after atom
      -------------------------
      $ - Line ends with atom
      \> - Word ends with atom
      
      Eg. ^The  - The line starts with The. The matches, Thesis also matches.
          \<cat - word starts with cat. such as... category, catalog, cat, ...
          \<cat\> - word starts with c follow by a and must end with t. exact match of word cat, not catalog.
      
      How to represent www.google.com using RE?
      \<w[3]\.go[2]gle\.com\>
      
 4. Extra
    \. represents single dot
    \\ represents \
    .* represents anything. eg. a.* = anything that starts with a.
